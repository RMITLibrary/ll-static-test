Converting between large and small units

How do you convert between units? Nurses use units that begin with milli and micro a lot. If you express your dosage in a smaller unit, the number must get bigger, and vice versa.

Find out how to convert dosages between larger and smaller units.

1. Convert the following amount to mcg: 0002.50000mg

Answer:
0002500.00mcg
The decimal point has moved to the right three times.

2. Convert the following amount to g: 0002.50000mg

Answer:
0.00250000g
The decimal point has moved to the left three times.

Answer the following questions related to converting between large and small units.

  1. How many grams are in 1 kg?
  2. How many grams are in 1 mg?
  3. How many grams are in 1 mcg?

Answers:

  1. One kg contains 1000 grams.
  2. One mg contains 1/1,000 grams.
  3. One mcg contains 1/1,000,000 grams.

Answer the following questions related to converting between large and small units.

  1. How many millilitres are in 27 kL?
  2. How many millilitres are in 27 L?
  3. How many millilitres are in 27 mcL?

Answers

  1. There are 27,000,000 mLs in 27 kL.
  2. There are 27,000 mLs in 27 L.
  3. There are 0.027 mLs in 27 mcL.
