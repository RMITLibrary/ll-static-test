Video

The video shortcode [ll-video], allows you to embed a video in a Learning lab page. A variety of aspect ratios are available.

Basic video

[ll-video url="https://path.to/video-embed"][transcript]<p>Transcript content here.</p>[/transcript][/ll-video]
  • url should either be in the form https://www.youtube.com/embed/video-id or https://youtu.be/video-id (as provided by the share button in youTube).
  • Each video has a margin of 40px applied top and bottom.
  • The max width for videos is 640px.
  • The max width for portrait videos is 400px.
  • Videos are aligned to the left by default.
  • All videos should include a transcript.

Video options

  • Use align="center" or align="centre" to centre the video.
  • Use caption="Insert caption here." to add a caption.
  • Use alert="Insert alert here." to add an alert bar. 
[ll-video url="https://path.to/video-embed" align="centre" caption="Insert caption here." alert="Alert message here"][transcript]<p>Transcript content here.</p>[/transcript][/ll-video]

Portrait video

Use portrait="true" to display a portrait video. The max width for portrait videos is 400px. The default aspect ratio is 16:9.

[ll-video url="https://path.to/video-embed" portrait="true"][transcript]<p>Transcript content here.</p>[/transcript][/ll-video]

Other aspect ratios

<!-- This is a 3-2 video -->
[ll-video url="https://path.to/video-embed" aspect="3-2"][transcript]<p>Transcript content here.</p>[/transcript][/ll-video]
<!-- This is a 4-3 video -->
[ll-video url="https://path.to/video-embed" aspect="4-3"][transcript]<p>Transcript content here.</p>[/transcript][/ll-video]
<!-- This is a square video -->
[ll-video url="https://path.to/video-embed" aspect="square"][transcript]<p>Transcript content here.</p>[/transcript][/ll-video]
